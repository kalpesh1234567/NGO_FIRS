How It Works · कसे वापरावे

From PDF to Excel
in 3 Steps

Simple enough for any NGO worker. No technical knowledge required.

1
Upload FIR PDFs
पीडीएफ फाइल अपलोड करा

Drag and drop one or multiple Maharashtra Police FIR PDFs onto the extractor. Supports IIF-I / NCRB standard format used across Maharashtra.

2
Click "Extract Data"
"माहिती काढा" वर क्लिक करा

The tool reads each PDF and automatically pulls out FIR number, date, police station, district, accused name, vehicle type, animal count, and applicable sections.

3
Download Excel
एक्सेल डाउनलोड करा

Review the extracted data in the table, then download as a formatted Excel file with both Marathi and English column headers — ready to share with your team or submit as a report.
