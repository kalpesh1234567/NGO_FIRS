About GauRaksha
NGO Platform

A digital case management system built specifically for Maharashtra NGOs working to protect cattle from illegal transport and slaughter.

महाराष्ट्रातील गोवंश संरक्षण संस्थांसाठी डिजिटल केस व्यवस्थापन प्रणाली

Our Mission

आमचे ध्येय

GauRaksha NGO works across Maharashtra to document, track, and legally pursue cases of illegal cattle transport and slaughter. This digital platform was built to replace manual Excel tracking and paper-based record-keeping with an efficient, automated system.

Every FIR registered under the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Act, Maharashtra Animal Preservation Act, and Motor Vehicle Act is digitally captured, indexed, and made searchable — helping NGO workers build stronger legal cases and identify repeat offenders.

⚖️

Legal Documentation · कायदेशीर दस्तऐवज

Every FIR is captured with full legal details including sections, accused, and evidence. Organized for court submissions.

🔍

Pattern Detection · नमुना ओळख

Dashboard analytics help identify repeat vehicles, accused, and routes used for illegal transport across districts.

📊

Reporting & Accountability · अहवाल

Generate district-wise and station-wise reports. Share data with authorities and partner organizations instantly.

🔒

Privacy First · गोपनीयता

All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server. Sensitive FIR data stays with you.

How the Technology Works

तंत्रज्ञान कसे काम करते

This is a fully client-side web application — meaning everything runs in your browser with no internet required after the initial load. Here's what powers it:

📄
PDF.js
Mozilla's library for reading PDF text in-browser
📊
SheetJS (XLSX)
Generates formatted Excel files client-side
📈
Chart.js
Interactive charts and data visualizations
💾
localStorage
Browser storage — data persists between sessions
🌐
Pure HTML/CSS/JS
No frameworks — works on any device
🎯
Regex Parsing
Smart extraction from Maharashtra IIF-I FIR format

How to Use This Platform

हे व्यासपीठ कसे वापरावे
  • Extract from PDFs: Go to "FIR Extractor", upload one or more Maharashtra Police FIR PDFs. Click "Extract Data" and the system will automatically pull all key fields. Then click "Save to Records" or download as Excel.
  • Add Manually: Go to "Add Record" if you don't have a PDF. Fill in the form fields in English — FIR No., date, police station, accused, vehicle, animals. Click Save.
  • View Dashboard: All saved records appear in the Dashboard with charts showing cases by station, animals per case, and cases over time. Use the search and filter to find specific records.
  • Export to Excel: From the Dashboard, click "Export All Excel" to download every record as a formatted .xlsx file with Marathi + English headers — matching your existing tracker format.
  • Data is always saved: Records are stored in your browser's local storage. They persist between sessions on the same device and browser. To transfer data, use the Excel export and re-import manually.
